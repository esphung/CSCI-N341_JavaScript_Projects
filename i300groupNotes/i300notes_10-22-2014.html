Recruiting

  • done by independent entity
  • can be through advertising
  • screeners
  • recruiting agencies

Before Testing

  • MUST HAVE EXTREMELY detailed checklist
    Seperate the tasks into different list
  • Prefer to have disclaimer
  • Perform dry runs

During Testing

  • make sure goals are SPECIFIC
  • do not give steps; do not give hints
  • must write at least 8x tasks of reasonable length
    ex. website testing - task: user subscribing
  • Hint: Put each task on cue cards and leave for participant to refer to after reading it to them
  • Can perform pre-test interview on participants if necessary (it is usually not, however)
  • Probes - questions to elicit a response from the particpant (try not to use "Why", "What" is more acceptable)
  • Encourage (ie: "Your giving us great data") the participant

  • take breaks
  • end the task early if need be
  • be honest to participant, yet tactful
  • remind participant that "We" are at fault, not them
